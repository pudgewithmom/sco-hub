About the Platform

Mission, concept and future of SCO Youth Talent Hub

The Problem

Many young participants of SCO programmes gain knowledge, connections and motivation, but after completing the programme they are often unsure where to apply their experience. On the other hand, leaders of youth initiatives frequently have ideas but lack a team: lawyers, analysts, translators, IT specialists, media professionals and project managers.

The Solution

SCO Youth Talent Hub is a digital platform that connects young professionals with leaders of project initiatives. It is a specialised space modelled on LinkedIn and project exchanges — but not for the mass labour market, rather for the SCO youth ecosystem.

Who It Is For

Specialists

Graduates and participants of SCO programmes who want to find real projects to apply their competencies.

Project Leaders

Initiative authors who need an international team to submit a grant or competition application.

Organisations

Educational institutions, youth councils, NGOs and expert centres looking for qualified young professionals.

How the Platform Can Grow

  • AI-powered recommendations for candidates and projects
  • Telegram bot for notifications
  • Database of grants, competitions and international programmes
  • Multilingual interface: Russian, English, Chinese
  • Integration with SCO educational programmes
  • Mentorship and expert support system
  • Automatic generation of project applications from templates
